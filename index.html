Frequently Asked Questions

Can this tool guarantee a win?

No tool can guarantee a lottery win. The lottery is a game of pure chance. This generator is designed to provide you with truly random numbers, removing human bias from your selection process.

How are the numbers generated?

We use your browser's native pseudorandom number generator (PRNG) to select numbers. The algorithm ensures that no duplicate numbers are chosen within the same draw, perfectly mimicking a real lottery machine.
